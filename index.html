Step 1 - Allocate Attributes

Spend your 7 points across the four Attributes. Maximum 3 points in any single Attribute. The class bonus in Step 4 adds +1 to one Attribute.

Attribute Rank Reference
0 Very Poor Even moderate tasks require help or skill investment to have average odds.
1 Average Decent odds on routine tasks; hard tasks require help or skill investment to maintain those odds.
2 Good Capable on most tasks, even hard ones, as long as you invest in relevant skills.
3 Great Can take on hard tasks without heavy skill investment, though extreme tasks still pose a challenge.
4 Natural Can handle most tasks, even extreme ones, with a bit of help or skill investment.
5 Grand Capable of dealing with extreme tasks with little skill investment.
6 Legendary With some help or skill investment, even near-impossible tasks are within reach.

Step 2 - Select Starting Skills

Pick 5 Skills to set at Rank 1. Your Class will add additional Skill bonuses in Step 4.

Selected: 0 / 5

Step 3 - Choose a Lineage

Your Lineage determines your size, Speed, languages, and special traits.

Select two Traditional Lineages. Choose which provides your Features, Major Trait, and Minor Trait.

Step 4 - Choose a Class

Your Class grants an Attribute bonus, Skill bonuses, and determines which Specialties you can pick.

Step 5 - Choose a Specialty

Your Specialty is your playstyle within your Class. You cannot change it later.

Step 6 - Starting Gear & Currency

Your Specialty determines the gear and currency you begin the game with.

Step 7 - Create a Lifepath

Click a row to select it, or type your own. These shape your character's backstory.

Step 8 - Status Points

Your stats are calculated automatically from your Attributes.

Step 9 - Record Talents

Write down the Talents gained from your Lineage, Specialty, and any General Talents you've selected. These appear on page 3 of your character sheet.

At Level 1 your Specialty Talent also grants you either a Martial Talent or Sorcery Talent.

Step 10 - Character Details

Fill in your character's name, appearance, and starting gear.

Level increases automatically in the Level Up step.

Step 11 - Export Character Sheet

Review your character, then export to the Realms of Legacy PDF character sheet — or continue to Level Up to advance beyond Level 1.

The PDF opens filled and ready to print or share.

Level Up

Advance one level at a time. Each level grants +7 Status Points (HP / MP), +2 Skill Points, and 1 Talent Point. Your Specialty Talent ranks up automatically at Levels 2, 6, 11, and 18.
